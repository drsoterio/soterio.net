Retro Futuristic Garden on a Living Canvas

An interactive art piece blending retro-futurism, robotics, and immersive design

Immersive Design, Robotics, Retro-Futurism

Step into a Retro Futuristic Garden on a Living Canvas, an interactive art installation that transports viewers into a world where a robot tenderly tends to a lush greenhouse and garden, set against a backdrop of stylized, futuristic architecture. This living canvas is a dynamic blend of art and technology, designed to engage and inspire.

The canvas comes alive with multiple interactive elements:

  • Activated Lights: Illuminating the “day” and “night” scenes, the lighting shifts to create a mesmerizing visual experience.
  • Motion Sensors: Triggering Neopixels along the frame, the sensors unveil a vibrant “day” scene. Flip the canvas upside down, and the scene transitions into a serene “night” view.
  • Interactive Robot: Pressing the button on the robot plays sound effects, adding an auditory layer to the immersive experience.

This project merges retro-futuristic aesthetics with cutting-edge technology, creating a space where art, nature, and innovation coexist. It invites viewers to explore the boundaries of imagination and interactivity, offering a glimpse into a future that feels both nostalgic and forward-thinking.
