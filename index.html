About Me

I am a chemist-turned-science-communicator pursuing a Doctoral Degree in Education at Columbia University. My work focuses on designing educational technologies, developing curricula, and democratizing invention. I hold an Honors Doctorate in Sciences from the University of São Paulo, with a collaborative research period at Cornell University. I have produced guidebooks, podcasts, science kits, co-created the Global Conversation on Sustainability with IUPAC, and founded Brazil’s first ACS Student Chapter, earning the ACS Leadership Award. My expertise spans instructional design, multimedia production, and creating innovative learning experiences.
