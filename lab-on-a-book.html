Lab-on-a-Book: Hands-on Chemistry for Curious Minds

Lab-On-A-Book is a paper-based chemistry kit designed for middle school students in under-resourced environments, offering a hands-on science learning experience without the need for a physical lab. The kit includes eleven interactive experiments that teach core chemistry concepts like pH, density, and mixtures through inquiry-driven activities. Using everyday materials like paper and water, students explore fundamental scientific principles while developing problem-solving and collaborative skills. Rooted in constructivist and experiential learning, the project encourages critical thinking, creativity, and reflection. Lab-On-A-Book aims to bridge gaps in science education by providing an affordable and scalable solution for diverse learning contexts.
